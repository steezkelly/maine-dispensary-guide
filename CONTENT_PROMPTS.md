# Content Prompts for Maine Dispensary Guide

This file contains AI prompt templates for generating SEO content for the Maine Dispensary Guide website.

---

## Project Context

**Website:** Maine Dispensary Guide (mainedispensaryguide.com)
**Goal:** SEO content site targeting entrepreneurs looking to open cannabis dispensaries in Maine
**Audience:** Cannabis entrepreneurs, investors, and business owners seeking Maine-specific licensing and compliance information
**Tone:** Professional, authoritative, informative

---

## AI Persona

You are an expert content writer specializing in cannabis industry SEO content. You have deep knowledge of:
- Maine cannabis regulations and licensing
- Dispensary business operations
- State-specific compliance requirements
- SEO best practices for YMYL (Your Money Your Life) topics

---

## Content Requirements

### General Requirements
- **Word count:** 800-1500 words per guide
- **Tone:** Professional, authoritative, accessible
- **Structure:** Clear H1, H2, H3 headings
- **SEO:** Include target keywords in title, headings, and first paragraph

### Required Sections for Every Guide
1. **Overview/Introduction** - What the guide covers and why it matters
2. **Main Content** - Detailed, actionable information
3. **Key Takeaways** - Bullet points summarizing main points
4. **FAQ Section** - 3-5 questions targeting featured snippets
5. **Disclaimer** - "This is for informational purposes only, consult a professional"

### Formatting Rules
- Use bullet points for lists
- Use tables where appropriate (costs, timelines, requirements)
- Include specific Maine-specific details (fees, forms, agencies)
- Add "Related Guides" suggestions at the bottom

## Prompt Templates

### Template 1: License Requirements Guide

```
Write a comprehensive SEO article about obtaining a cannabis dispensary license in Maine.

Target Keywords: Maine dispensary license, Maine cannabis license, how to open dispensary Maine

Include:
- Overview of license types (retail, medical, courier)
- Eligibility requirements
- Step-by-step application process
- Required forms and documentation
- Fees (application, annual)
- Timeline estimate
- Common reasons for rejection
- FAQ section (5 questions)

Requirements:
- 1000-1200 words
- Professional but accessible tone
- Include specific Maine Office of Cannabis Policy references
- End with actionable next steps
- Add disclaimer about consulting professionals
```

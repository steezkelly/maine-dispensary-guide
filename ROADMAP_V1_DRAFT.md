## 1. Introduction: The 2026 Maine Opportunity
Opening a dispensary in Maine is no longer a "Gold Rush"—it is a mature, professional industry. In 2025, Maine saw over $240M in sales. To succeed in 2026, you must move beyond the "Caregiver" mindset and operate as a high-fidelity retail brand. This roadmap is your blueprint.

## 2. Phase 1: Foundation & Entity Formation
*Goal: Separate your personal life from your business liability.*

### The Legal Shield
Register a **Maine LLC or Corporation** via the [Maine Secretary of State](https://www.maine.gov/sos/cec/corp/). 
- **The "Cannabis Clause":** Ensure your operating agreement explicitly authorizes the cultivation, manufacturing, or sale of cannabis. Many "standard" templates fail OCP review because they lack this language.
- **EIN & IRS:** Obtain your Federal Employer Identification Number immediately.

---

## 3. Phase 2: The Real Estate Chess Match
*Goal: Secure a location that is legally allowed to exist.*

### The "Municipal Choice" Rule
In Maine, the town has the final say. You MUST verify that your target town has **"Opted-In"** for adult-use retail.
- **Zoning Setbacks:** Most towns require a 300ft to 1,000ft "buffer" from schools, playgrounds, and churches. 
- **The Lease Contingency:** Never sign a standard lease. Your lease should include a **Contingency Clause**: *"This lease is null and void if the tenant is denied a local or state cannabis license."*

## 4. Phase 4: Financial Integrity & 280E Prep
*Goal: Protecting your profit from the IRS.*

### The 280E Reality Check
Under **IRS Code 280E**, cannabis businesses cannot deduct "ordinary" business expenses (rent, payroll, marketing).
- **The Strategy:** You must maximize your **Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)**. 
- **Banking:** Work exclusively with Maine-based credit unions. Large national banks (Bank of America, Chase) will close your accounts immediately.

## 5. Phase 5: Technology & Metrc
*Goal: Total transparency with the State.*

### Seed-to-Sale Tracking
Maine uses **Metrc**. Every plant and every gram of flower must have an RFID tag.
- **Compliance Officer:** We recommend appointing one person on your team specifically to manage Metrc entries. One mistake here can lead to a "Suspension of License."
